Ubuntu One Music

With Ubuntu One Mobile Music Streaming you can stream your entire music library from your personal cloud to your Android device.

To get started, go to one.ubuntu.com and sign up for the free Ubuntu One Basic package. Add the Ubuntu One Mobile package for a low monthly or annual price. Try it free for 30 days.

The Ubuntu One Mobile package includes the following features.

  • Stream your entire music library from your personal cloud to iPhone and Android devices, powered by Subsonic.
  • Unlimited hours of music streaming. Works best over Wi-Fi.
  • Excellent companion to the Ubuntu One Music Store.
  • Includes photos and contacts sync for iPhone and Android devices.
